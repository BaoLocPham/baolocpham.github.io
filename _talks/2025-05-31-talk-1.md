You may be familiar with the monthly AI Meetup events organized by Menlo Research in Singapore, which always attract great interest from the community. With the desire to promote the development of the Open Source and AI communities in Vietnam, Menlo Research is excited to bring this event to Ho Chi Minh City.


AI Meetup HCMC is a great place to:
- Connect and expand your network with like-minded individuals and professionals in the AI field.
- Stay updated on the latest advancements and research from individuals, startups, and research teams.
- Showcase your products, research, or AI projects to the community.
- Explore opportunities for collaboration, employment, or internships in the promising AI/Tech industry.

The event aims to build a strong and connected AI community in Ho Chi Minh City, providing a platform for startups and small research teams to easily connect and gain wider recognition.
Join us to share your contributions and work together to advance the development of AI!
